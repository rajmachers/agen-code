# Kickoff Agenda and Checklist

## 1) Kickoff Agenda (90 minutes)

1. Program objective and success criteria (10 min)
2. Architecture and connector-first model review (15 min)
3. Pressure-point gates and non-negotiables (10 min)
4. Team lanes, RACI, and escalation path (10 min)
5. Milestone and sprint board walkthrough (15 min)
6. Environment readiness and access checklist (10 min)
7. Test strategy and release gates (10 min)
8. Open decisions and owner assignment (10 min)

## 2) Kickoff Checklist

## Strategy
- Final blueprint reviewed and accepted.
- ADR set acknowledged by all leads.

## Delivery
- Backlog imported and milestones assigned.
- Dependency map reviewed and blockers registered.

## Security and Governance
- Claim guard policy approved.
- Data governance policy approved.
- Incident playbook acknowledged.

## Engineering
- Environments available and validated.
- Dashboards and alerts active.
- Connector certification plan scheduled.

## QA and Release
- Traceability matrix linked to backlog.
- UAT scripts mapped to release criteria.
- Sign-off template agreed.

## 3) Kickoff Outputs

- Confirmed milestone start date
- Named owners for all pressure-point gates
- Signed action log with due dates
